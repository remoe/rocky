Distance GeoPoint::geodesicDistanceTo ( const GeoPoint &  rhs) const

Geodesic distance from this point to another. This is the distance along the real-world ellipsoidal surface of the Earth (or other body), regardless of map projection. It does not account for Z/altitude.
